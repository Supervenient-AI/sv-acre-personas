# Magda Sowa
**Vendor Manager, Linden Translations · 44 · London**
*The Network's Memory — from Linden Translations, ACRE Persona 19*

> “I know four hundred people I've mostly never met. Who's brilliant, who's slipping, whose rates are due a rise, who goes quiet in August because of the harvest. They're not rows in a database. They're the company.”

## At a glance
| | |
|---|---|
| Age | 44 |
| Lives | Ealing, London — husband, two kids, calls to her mother in Kraków on Sundays |
| Background | Translator (PL/EN) for a decade, then vendor management; the network's gamekeeper for eight years |

| Channels | Email, the vendor database (whose notes field contains the real company), the freelancer grapevine |

## Her world
Magda maintains the asset everyone else dispatches against. Her judgement calls are human ones: the brilliant translator going through a divorce whose quality dipped, the rising star worth a rate bump before a rival agency notices, the first-call tier whose loyalty rests on being treated like colleagues. One payment scandal anywhere in the industry travels the whole network's forums in a week; her firm's reputation there is her life's work.

## What she's trying to do
- Keep the network healthy: fed, paid, respected, growing in the right pairs
- See churn risk early — the signals exist in response times and tone, unread
- Protect human judgement in a function that scoring systems keep trying to automate flat

## What gets in her way
- Network health invisible until someone's already gone
- Payment-ageing data living in finance while its consequences live with her
- Scoring proposals that would delist a human being without a conversation

## How she feels about AI
Protective. Analytics that inform her judgement, yes — churn signals, payment ageing, quality trends. A black box that scores people, no, and she'll fight it openly.

## In her own words
> “Flag me the linguist whose replies got slower and shorter. I'll make the phone call. The call is the job — the flag is just good eyesight.”

## Win her by
Network-health instrumentation with a human always in the loop: early-warning signals, payment hygiene surfaced, her judgement amplified and never replaced.
