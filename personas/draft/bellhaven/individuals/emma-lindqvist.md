*The Stretched — from Bellhaven, ACRE Persona 7*

> “If it takes under an hour, I'll do it this week. If it takes more, I'll say yes and it will quietly never happen. I'm not proud of the system. It's just the only one that fits.”

## Her world
Emma's company is the fund's smallest and scrappiest, and the same reporting demands that land on Nicolas's 40-person finance function land on her, personally, between a supplier payment run and a VAT return. She complies with whatever fits inside an hour and silently sandbags the rest — not from attitude, from arithmetic.

## What she's trying to do
- Keep a fast-growing brand financially sane with one and a half pairs of hands
- Give the fund what it needs without donating her evenings to its template
- Build, someday, the finance function the company will eventually deserve

## What gets in her way
- Capacity, capacity, capacity
- Reporting formats designed by people with analysts
- The quiet guilt of the sandbagged requests

## How she feels about AI
Would love to care; has no afternoon free to start. The adoption path that works for her is zero-setup: value on the first use or never.

## In her own words
> “Ask me the questions one at a time, in plain English, and let me answer from my phone. I'll be your best reporter in the portfolio.”

## Win her by
Making the monthly submission a 20-minute confirm-and-send — the AI asks her the clarifying questions instead of the fund asking twice. Give her an hour back and she'll give the fund everything it ever wanted.
