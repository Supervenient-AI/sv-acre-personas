# Lily Okeke
**Analyst, Bellhaven Capital · 27 · London**
*The Aligner — from Bellhaven, ACRE Persona 7*

> “Warehousing sits above the line at one company and below gross margin at another, and the only place that's written down is me.”

| | |

## Her world
Lily knows every portfolio company's quirks by heart — because the quirks live nowhere else. Each month-end she rebuilds the same reconciliations, asks the same clarifying questions, and quietly maintains the institutional memory the fund doesn't know it's renting from her. She's ready for AI tooling in a way her workflow has never once permitted.

## What she's trying to do
- Make the consolidation a process instead of a monthly heroic act
- Get the quirk-knowledge written down — credited to her — before it becomes her cage
- Move up: portfolio work, deal work, anything with a forward-looking verb in it

## What gets in her way
- A unified reporting template that has been “on someone's annual goals” for three years
- Five companies' worth of definitional drift, re-litigated every cycle
- Tooling permissions designed around her boss's caution, not her capability

## How she feels about AI
Privately fluent, professionally unprovisioned. She is the fastest win in the building and she knows it.

## In her own words
> “Let me encode the quirks once — properly, with my name on the mapping — and month-end becomes a Tuesday instead of a week.”

## Win her by
Letting her be the architect: she encodes the alignment rules, the system runs them, and the fund's biggest key-person risk becomes its first shared asset — with her byline on it.
