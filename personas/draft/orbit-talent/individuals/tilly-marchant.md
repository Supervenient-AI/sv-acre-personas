# Tilly Marchant
**Talent Manager, Orbit Talent · 26 · Peckham**
*The Knot With a Heart — from Orbit, ACRE Persona 37*

> “Seven creators, four hundred WhatsApps a day. I'm agent, project manager, translator and — at 11pm before a big post — therapist. The brand's committee sends six contradictory notes; what reaches Kayla at midnight is one kind, clear paragraph. That translation is the job. Nobody invoices for it.”

## Her world
Tilly's day is the matchmaker double-load plus care work: brand-side choreography (briefs, contracts, approval rounds) and creator-side everything (schedules, invoices, confidence wobbles, the algorithm's mood swings). Her creators trust her with their careers and their 1am anxieties; her margin for either depends on the admin layer shrinking.

## What she's trying to do
- Keep seven careers ascending and seven humans intact — they're the same job done in different hours
- Consolidate the approval chaos: notes translated, time-shifted, anchored
- Make the care work visible — it's the agency's retention engine and her promotion case

## What gets in her way
- Brand notes arriving raw, plural and contradictory at creator bedtimes
- Payment tails: 60–90 days of brand AP process, chased on her relationship capital
- The admin layer eating the hours the humans actually need

## How she feels about AI
Native and surgical: automate the chasing, consolidation and scheduling — touch the relationship thread and she's gone, taking seven creators' trust with her.

## In her own words
> “Translate the committee, time-shift the delivery, chase the invoices with something that has no feelings to spend. I'll keep the feelings. The feelings are the moat.”

## Win her by
The admin layer evaporated and the midnight-notes problem ended — her WhatsApp kept human, everything around it made machinery.
