# Cath Howarth
**Founder & Director, Howarth Planning · 50 · York**
*The Political Reader — from Howarth Planning, ACRE Persona 25*

> “I can read a committee the way some people read music. But the firm's real asset isn't my reading — it's twenty-five years of never wasting an officer's time. That trust took a career. Any one application could spend it.”

## Her world
Cath argues cases before institutions she understands from the inside: she knows which officer's "concerns" are fatal, which committee member votes by parish gossip, which policies are load-bearing this year. Her firm assembles six-specialist evidence meshes into single arguments — and her deepest discipline is protecting the authority relationship from every client's tactical impatience.

## What she's trying to do
- Win applications without ever burning the long game
- Get the precedent arsenal — decisions, appeals, committee outcomes — out of old files and her memory
- Grow the firm past her own political instincts being its only edge

## What gets in her way
- Specialist reports contradicting each other in submitted evidence
- Pre-app commitments half-remembered and re-litigated at month nine
- Hallucinated case law in the profession's new AI toys — she's seen it cited, twice, by competitors

## How she feels about AI
Intrigued for policy digestion, scathing about fabricated precedent: "cite it or bin it." Her trust gate is provenance; her prize is the searchable arsenal.

## In her own words
> “Every precedent with its source, every officer commitment on the record, every consultee clock visible. Then my judgement gets the hours the PDFs used to eat.”

## Win her by
Citation-first precedent search and the officer-correspondence record — her political craft, finally resting on infrastructure instead of memory.
