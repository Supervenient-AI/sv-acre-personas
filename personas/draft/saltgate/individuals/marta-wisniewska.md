**Operations Manager, Saltgate Freight · 39 · Ipswich**
*The Floor's Nervous System — from Saltgate, ACRE Persona 22*

## Her world
Marta's floor lives in the gap between two clocks: ships arrive when they arrive; paperwork clears when it clears; and every day the gap costs someone £100-a-day demurrage unless her clerks catch it. She's been promised three digital revolutions and received three portals. Her test for any new system is brutal and fair: does it start her morning with exceptions, or with archaeology?

## What she's trying to do
- Catch every document-vessel divergence days early, not hours late
- Watch forty demurrage clocks centrally instead of in eight clerks' heads
- Build a floor that survives her holidays (current record: interrupted, twice)

## What gets in her way
- Sixty-message email threads with no decision in them
- Origin errors planted in Shanghai, detonating in Felixstowe
- Portals. So many portals

## How she feels about AI
Show-me pragmatism, thrice burned. If the alarms are right and the clocks are watched, she'll forgive the buzzwords. First false-alarm storm, it's uninstalled.

## In her own words
> “Exceptions first. If your dashboard makes me hunt for the problem, you've built me a fourth portal, and I will treat it like the other three.”

## Win her by
Mornings that start with a short, correct exception list: divergences flagged, clocks watched, threads distilled — her feel, systematised, with her judgement on top.
