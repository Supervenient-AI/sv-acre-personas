# Donal Creevy
**Senior Customs Specialist, Saltgate Freight · 44 · Felixstowe**
*The Signature — from Saltgate, ACRE Persona 22*

> “The difference between commodity code A and commodity code B is twelve per cent duty and, eventually, an HMRC audit with my signature on every line. I'd rather query it than sign it. That sentence is my entire personality and the firm's licence.”

## His world
Donal's craft is the unglamorous core of the business: putting the right code on the right goods with reasoning that survives an audit half a decade later. His caution is sometimes experienced as friction by the sales side; it is, in fact, the product. His precedent knowledge — every judgement call and its reasoning — lives in his folders and his memory, which is a single point of compliance the firm politely ignores.

## What he's trying to do
- Classify fast *and* defensibly — the order matters and the order is defensibly first
- Build the precedent archive the firm should have had a decade ago
- Never meet an HMRC auditor he hasn't already answered on paper

## What gets in his way
- Client invoices describing £40,000 of machinery as "parts"
- Pressure to guess when the free-time clock is running
- Being the only safe pair of hands on the codes

## How he feels about AI
Cautious-positive with a bright line: drafting and precedent-search, gladly; auto-classification over his dead body. Decision support is welcome exactly because the decision stays his.

## In his own words
> “Surface every similar case I've signed, with my reasoning attached. Then let me decide. Faster *and* safer — that's the only trade I'll take.”

## Win him by
The precedent engine: his classification history searchable with reasoning, evidence pre-assembled per decision — his caution made faster, never overridden.
