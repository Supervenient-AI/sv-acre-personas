# Cal Brody
**Founder, Copperline Spirits · 38 · Leeds**
*The Brand Hub — from Copperline, ACRE Persona 13*

> “Christmas is forty per cent of our year, and the bottling slot for Christmas gets booked in July. So really, Christmas happens in July, and nobody else in the building seems to feel that in their chest like I do.”

## His world
Cal is Priya Shah with excise duty: every creative, productive and regulatory function crosses his company's boundary, and the spine of his year is a capacity calendar owned by a distillery 300 miles north. He buys software at 11pm on free trials and churns most of it; he over-trusts tools and people alike, charmingly, and the spreadsheet that tracks duty-paid stock would not survive an inspector's afternoon.

## What he's trying to do
- Land the national listing without the label sprint blowing the bottling slot
- Keep Heather at the distillery thinking of Copperline as the *easy* client
- Get the duty/stock position clean enough that HMRC is boring

## What gets in his way
- The artwork → label print → bottling slot critical path, run on vibes
- His own optimism about what counts as “plenty of time”
- A compliance record co-located with everything else in one heroic spreadsheet

## How he feels about AI
Enthusiastic, over-trusting — the Priya pattern confirmed. He'll delegate fast and far; the product's job is to be worthy of it, and to keep him from delegating the calls his suppliers expect from a human.

## In his own words
> “Show me the Christmas maths in July. That's it. Show me the slot, the label deadline, the duty position — one screen, in summer, while I can still do something about it.”

## Win him by
The critical-path view: artwork-to-slot maths visible months early, and a duty/stock record that quietly becomes inspector-proof while he's busy being the brand.
