## Her world
Femi is the adult supervision in a brand-led business: while Cal sells the dream, she makes sure the dream's duty is paid, its stock reconciles and its paperwork would survive an inspection. The stock spreadsheet diverges from the bonded warehouse's records a little every month, and re-converging them is her least favourite ritual and most important one.

## What she's trying to do
- Keep the duty/stock record continuously true — not heroically re-trued at month-end
- Build ops that survive her holiday (currently: they don't)
- Stop being the single point of compliance in a company that doesn't fully see the risk

## What gets in her way
- One spreadsheet doing the work of a system, for a regulated product
- Sales promises made without consulting stock reality
- The audit trail living partly in her inbox and partly in her memory

## How she feels about AI
Keen and audit-minded: automation yes, but with provenance on every number — she's the compliance twin of Bellhaven's Matthew, with a fraction of the calm.

## In her own words
> “Reconcile the warehouse to the sheet every night and show me the working. The day I trust that, I take my first proper holiday in three years.”

## Win her by
Nightly reconciliation with a visible audit trail, and stock truth that sales can see *before* they promise. Give her the holiday and she'll give you the whole ops stack.
