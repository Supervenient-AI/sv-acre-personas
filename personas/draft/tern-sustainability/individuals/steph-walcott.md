# Steph Walcott
**Head of Sustainability (client-side) · 39**
*The Conscripted Relay — from Tern, ACRE Persona 32*

> “I'm a department of one with a board mandate, a retailer's deadline, and no budget. Tern needs supplier data; procurement guards the supplier relationships; and every chasing email goes out under *my* name, spending *my* credibility. I am the campaign. I'd just like to also have a job.”

## Her world
Steph hired Tern to do what she can't alone — and discovered the model still runs through her: procurement won't let consultants contact suppliers directly, so every request, reminder and apology travels under her signature. Her internal capital is the campaign's true budget, and it's depleting faster than the carbon is.

## What she's trying to do
- Hit the retailer's portal deadline — the mandate that funds everything else
- Get the supplier campaign run *for* her, not *through* her
- Convert sustainability from her solo crusade into the company's habit

## What gets in her way
- Procurement's territorial guard on supplier relationships
- Chasing emails spending credibility she needs for the board
- Burnout — the sector's occupational epidemic, visible from here

## How she feels about AI
Desperate-pragmatic; already improvising with ChatGPT. A governed surface that drafts in her voice and sequences the chases sparingly would be the first staff she's ever had.

## In her own words
> “Run the campaign under my name without consuming my name. Draft like me, chase politely, escalate rarely — and give procurement nothing to glare about.”

## Win her by
Campaign automation that spends her capital like the scarce currency it is — and a portal-ready output that makes her board slide write itself.
