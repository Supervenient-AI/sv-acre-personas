# Dr Sofia Brandt
**R&D Director, Voltaic Edge & AURELIA Project Lead · 42 · Coventry**
*The Unempowered Conductor — from AURELIA, ACRE Persona 26*

> “I lead a consortium in which nobody reports to me, the funder governs without coordinating, and the quarterly claim requires four finance systems to agree. I coordinate by diplomacy because I have literally nothing else.”

## Her world
Sofia runs two parallel realities: the project's *true state* (lab results, partner drift, the post-doc's looming departure) and its *reported state* (the funder's deliverable schedule, maintained with managed optimism). The gap between them is professional judgement, not dishonesty — but it lives entirely in her head, alongside the diplomatic ledger of who owes whom what across four organisations.

## What she's trying to do
- Deliver the project — the science and the paperwork, in that order of love and reverse order of hours
- Make the quarterly claim a process instead of a 90-day heroic scramble
- Harvest Chen Wei's knowledge before his contract walks it out the door

## What gets in her way
- Authority and articulation living in different places — the funder governs, she coordinates, nobody compels
- The publication/IP collision course, re-run per paper
- Trade-secret chemistry and academic openness sharing one shared folder

## How she feels about AI
Pragmatic with a perimeter: funder-report drafting yes, the secret chemistry never. Her dream is mundane and enormous — claims-readiness as a continuous state.

## In her own words
> “Keep the true record and the reported record both honest, and show me the gap. The gap is my job. Everything else can be machinery.”

## Win her by
Continuous claims assembly, partner-state visibility without chasing, and a scheduled knowledge harvest against the post-doc clock — the unempowered conductor, finally instrumented.
