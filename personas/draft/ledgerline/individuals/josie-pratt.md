# Josie Pratt
**Senior Bookkeeper, Ledgerline · 34 · Leigh**
*The Quirk Holder — from Ledgerline, ACRE Persona 30*

> “Twelve clients who all think they're my only one. I know that Gary's ‘CASH £400' means a different thing every time, that the café's Saturday takings always go missing, and that the haulier's fuel cards double-post. None of that is written anywhere. It's just... me.”

## Her world
Josie translates chaos into ledgers: receipt photos taken at red lights, invoices described from memory, the monthly archaeology of "what's this payment?" Her patience is professional-grade and her query list is sisyphean — questions raised, half-answered, resurrected at year-end by accountants with billable curiosity. The chase is the job's hidden majority, and Gary alone is a part-time role.

## What she's trying to do
- Close twelve months a month without her evenings subsidising other people's filing
- Kill the query graveyard: questions answered once, at the time, in context
- Get the quirk knowledge recorded — credited — before it becomes her cage

## What gets in her way
- Clients who supply records at deadline-minus-two-days
- Queries that die unanswered and resurrect expensively
- Being interruptible by forty per cent of her phone

## How she feels about AI
Comfortable and ready — anything that chases Gary warmly so she doesn't have to, and that asks him one plain-English question at a time instead of her list of nine.

## In her own words
> “Encode the quirks with my name on them, chase in my tone, and let questions die only by being answered. I'll do the books. The books were never the problem.”

## Win her by
Chase automation in her voice, in-context query resolution, and the quirk codex credited to her — the month-end finally depending on systems instead of glove boxes.
