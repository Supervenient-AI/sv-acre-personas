# Bev Onslow
**Payroll Specialist, Ledgerline · 53 · Wigan**
*The Zero-Tolerance Pulse — from Ledgerline, ACRE Persona 30*

> “Twenty-five years of payroll and I have never paid anyone wrongly. Not once. People think that's a system. It's not. It's a refusal — I will not run a payroll on an unconfirmed timesheet, and I don't care whose deadline is crying about it.”

## Her world
Bev's work touches rent, groceries and court-ordered deductions — the edge of the business where errors reach actual humans on actual Fridays. Her edge-case knowledge (whose maternity overlaps which court order, which client's overtime rules contradict their own contracts) is the firm's least documented, highest-stakes asset. Her rigidity is famous, resented at deadline, and absolutely correct.

## What she's trying to do
- Keep the streak: every payslip right, every RTI on time, forever
- Get timesheets in *before* the chase becomes a standoff
- Write down the edge-cases — she's 53 and the streak shouldn't retire with her

## What gets in her way
- "Just run it, I'll confirm after" — the request she's refused a thousand times
- Edge-case knowledge living in her head and a locked cabinet
- Clients' casualness about the most consequential numbers she handles

## How she feels about AI
Deeply suspicious near the payslip, open everywhere upstream of it. Her terms are exact: propose everything, file nothing unconfirmed — automation that ends at her nod.

## In her own words
> “Get the timesheets in on time and draft what you like. But nothing — *nothing* — runs without my nod. The streak isn't stubbornness. The streak is someone's rent.”

## Win her by
Timesheet chasing that lands confirmations before she asks, edge-cases documented under her authorship, and a hard human gate on every run — her refusal, productised as the feature it always was.
