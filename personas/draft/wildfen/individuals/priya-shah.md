**Founder, Wildfen Oat Co. · 34 · London**
*The Brand Hub — from Wildfen, ACRE Persona 1*

> “Everything's in my head. The brief, the deadlines, who's waiting on who. People say that like it's impressive. It's actually the thing that's going to sink us.”

## Her world
Priya runs on momentum and caffeine. Her day is a collage: a buyer call at 9, a dieline question at 11, investor emails at lunch, and a packaging crisis whenever it likes. She has 14 apps with notifications on and trusts none of them — the real system of record is her memory, and she can feel it fraying. She loves this company like a third parent loves a startup: fiercely, and at cost.

## What she's trying to do
- Land the Sainsbury's listing — the company-maker — without the packaging sprint blowing up
- Stop being the bottleneck: every decision, file and chase currently routes through her
- Get one evening a week back before her partner stages an intervention

## What gets in her way
- Five brilliant collaborators in four cities who've mostly never spoken to each other
- A brief that exists in four subtly different remembered versions
- Her own optimism: she treats every revision as free and every deadline as survivable

## How she feels about AI
Enthusiastic to a fault — she already drafts investor emails with a chatbot and would happily hand it the keys. The risk isn't resistance; it's over-delegation: she'll let it make calls her freelancers expect from a human.

## In her own words
> “I don't need another app. I need the evening back. Show me the evening.”

## Win her by
Giving her a week-two moment she can tell another founder about at a trade show: one evening saved, one crisis that didn't happen, zero new admin.
