# Sophie Okafor
**Account Director, Marrow & Co · 41 · Leeds**
*The Shadow Coordinator — from Wildfen, ACRE Persona 1*

> “I love Priya. I do. But I am not a project manager for freelancers I didn't hire, on hours nobody's paying for.”

|---|---|

## Her world
Sophie is professionally warm and privately precise. She's seen every kind of client and every kind of chaos, and she protects her agency's process the way other people protect their weekends — which she also protects. Lately she's been keeping a quiet tally of the unpaid hours she spends chasing Priya's freelancers, and the tally is starting to show in her tone.

## What she's trying to do
- Keep Marrow & Co's ownership of the Wildfen brand relationship visibly intact
- Protect scope: “we are not moving platforms for a £30k account”
- Get the quality of freelance artwork up without her feedback arriving third-hand and softened

## What gets in her way
- Unpaid project management that was never in the retainer
- Out-of-hours pings and scope creep treated as free
- A founder who routes everything through herself, badly

## How she feels about AI
Sceptical-professional. She's watched “game-changing” tools come and go for two decades. She'll give a new product exactly one week to prove it respects Marrow's process rather than flattening it — and she'll be right about whichever way it goes.

## In her own words
> “If it makes the chasing disappear and leaves the creative relationship alone, fine. If it tries to *be* the relationship, we're done.”

## Win her by
Making the unpaid coordination vanish from her week while her agency's role stays visibly hers. Bonus points if it never, ever messages her at 9pm.
