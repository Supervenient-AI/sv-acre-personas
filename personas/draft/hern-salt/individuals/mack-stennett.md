# Mack Stennett
**Production Manager, Hern & Salt · 39 · Bristol**
*The City in His Head — from Hern & Salt, ACRE Persona 40*

> “I know where the cables ran last year, which gate floods, and why stage two's load-in has to beat the water truck. None of that is written anywhere. Build week, I sleep in a cabin and answer the same 5am questions I answered last July — because the answers live in me, and I almost didn't come back this season.”

## His world
Mack's craft is sequencing physics: forty suppliers' trucks, cranes and crews converging on a field in an order only he fully holds. He's watched event tech die in mud and pitches alike; his scepticism is earned. But the 5am questions, the re-derived cable maps, the slippage ripples computed in his head at midnight — he knows exactly what should hold those instead of him.

## What he's trying to do
- Build the city on time, in weather, without heroics becoming the plan
- Bank the site folklore — credited, his — before his knees make the decision for him
- Halve build week's surprises: the 5am class specifically

## What gets in his way
- One supplier's slipped day re-sequencing thirty-nine others, visible only in his head
- Wrap amnesia deleting each year's hard-won site truth
- Tools that demand attention from hands that are holding a radio and a cable plan

## How he feels about AI
Mud-tested sceptic: show him slippage ripples computed live and last year's site notes surfacing at the right map square, and he'll concede — gruffly, permanently.

## In his own words
> “The schedule that re-ripples itself when a truck's late, and the site's memory under my name instead of just under my hat. That's the product. Everything else is a lanyard with batteries.”

## Win him by
The living build schedule plus the banked site folklore — build week's surprises reduced to weather, his knowledge made the firm's asset with his name still on it.
