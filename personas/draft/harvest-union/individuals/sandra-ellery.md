# Sandra Ellery
**Buying Coordinator, Harvest Union · 47 · Hebden Bridge**
*The Deal Maker — from Harvest Union, ACRE Persona 33*

> “I negotiate with forty suppliers on volumes I can project but never promise — sixty independent shops will order what they order. The suppliers know it. I know they know it. The whole dance is performed on arithmetic neither side can quite see.”

## At a glance
| | |
|---|---|
| Age | 47 |
| Lives | Mytholmroyd — husband, two teenagers, an allotment that supplies her own kitchen better than some members order |
| Background | Buying roles at a wholesaler, then a health-food chain; joined the co-op for the ethics, stayed for the chess |
| Role | Negotiates the group's supplier deals — the leverage that is the entire point of the federation |
| Craft | Supplier folklore: who flexes on thresholds, who's bluffing on minimums, whose "final offer" has two more rounds in it |
| Channels | Negotiation calls, trade shows, the deal sheets members skim, the ordering platform's under-read truth |
| Devices | Laptop of deal models, a phone of supplier reps' direct lines, a notebook of remembered concessions |

## Her world
Sandra's deals fund the federation: hit the volume thresholds and the rebate proves membership's worth; miss them — because members ordered off-group out of habit or impatience — and her negotiating position erodes invisibly. The ordering platform holds the volume truth that would arm her; analysing it has been politically untouchable since the day someone said "surveillance" at a council meeting.

## What she's trying to do
- Win deals that make sixty shops' membership self-evidently worthwhile
- Get volume truth at the negotiating table — projections she can defend
- Capture the folklore: she's 47 and the concession history lives in her notebook

## What gets in her way
- Leverage leakage: members' off-group ordering dissolving the thresholds quietly
- Projections built on hope where data exists but consent doesn't
- Suppliers' divide-and-conquer instincts, professionally applied

## How she feels about AI
Practical: deal-history at the table and volume truth (consented) would be worth actual margin points. The mechanism is irrelevant; the arithmetic is everything.

## In her own words
> “Show each member their own share of each threshold — ‘your orders are 4% of the discount you're enjoying' — and the leakage fixes itself. No mandate. Just mirrors.”

## Win her by
Member-facing threshold arithmetic (the mirror, not the mandate) and the deal-history engine — her folklore banked, her projections armed.
