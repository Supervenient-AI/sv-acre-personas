## Her world
Priti sees fragments: a media list here, an urgent press release there, never the whole account. She's senior enough to be useful from cold — that's why they book her — but the cold start costs everyone, every time. She'd take more interesting work from H&V if it ever arrived with its context attached.

## What she's trying to do
- Be valuable from minute one of a two-day month
- Get the strategic work, not just the overflow reports
- Keep her calendar diversified — no single client over a third of her income

## What gets in her way
- Briefs without context, context without briefs
- Being structurally last to know anything about accounts she's writing for
- Client-side tools that arrive as conscription and feel like surveillance

## How she feels about AI
Fluent — she uses it across all her clients for research and first drafts. Her line: tools that hand her context, yes; tools that watch her hours, no.

## In her own words
> “Send me a brief that's actually self-contained and I'll deliver same-day. I'm not slow. I'm uninformed.”

## Win her by
Briefs that arrive complete — background, voice, history, deliverable — so her first billable minute is a productive one.
