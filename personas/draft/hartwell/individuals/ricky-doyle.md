# Ricky Doyle
**Estate Agent · 34 · Nottingham**
*The Improvised Coordinator — from Hartwell, ACRE Persona 20*

> “Every Friday I ring every party in every chain I've got. The solicitors think I'm a nuisance. I'm also the only person who knows the seller's getting divorced and the buyer upstairs just lost his mortgage. Who's the nuisance now?”

## His world
Ricky is the chain's accidental project manager — the least qualified, most motivated party, performing weekly the coordination no one else owns. His commission dies with the chain, so he pumps information through it by sheer telephonic force. The solicitors can't formally use his gossip; his gossip is nonetheless the most predictive data in the constellation.

## What he's trying to do
- Complete: his income is literally the chain's survival rate
- Spot the wobbling transaction early enough to manage it (a new buyer lined up, a nudge to a lender)
- Be the agent solicitors don't dread — referrals run both ways

## What gets in his way
- Solicitors who (rightly) won't tell him things and (wrongly) tell him nothing
- Chains stalling invisibly two links beyond his reach
- A reputation for pushiness that is also his entire function

## How he feels about AI
Instant, opportunistic adopter of anything that closes deals faster. He'll be the loudest evangelist in the county the week a tool demonstrably saves a chain.

## In his own words
> “Give me momentum signals — green, amber, red per transaction — and I'll do what I do. I don't need the details. I need the weather.”

## Win him by
Chain-momentum visibility at his legitimate level: enough signal to act and brag, with the legal layer sealed off — the accelerant given a dashboard instead of a phone book.
