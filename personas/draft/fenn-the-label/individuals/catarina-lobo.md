# Catarina Lobo
**Production Manager, Porto factory · 41 · Porto**
*The Queue Sovereign — from Fenn the Label, ACRE Persona 12*

> “I like Fenn. The clothes are beautiful and Saskia is kind. But when the big label's order and the small label's order want the same machines, kindness is not a production schedule.”

## Her world
Catarina manages the gap between a dozen labels' dreams and one factory's hours. Her affection for Fenn is real and strictly rationed: late tech packs, panicked changes and chased approvals make a label expensive to love, and when capacity tightens, the expensive ones slip. The labels never see this ledger. It runs anyway.

## What she's trying to do
- Keep the floor running at full, calm capacity — chaos costs more than overtime
- Reward the clients who arrive complete and on time, because they're the ones who make her week work
- Protect her machinists from other people's planning failures

## What gets in her way
- Tech packs with ambiguity sewn in — every unclear measurement becomes her floor's problem
- Instructions in idiomatic midnight English, executed differently in Portuguese daylight
- WhatsApp chaos aimed at her personally because nobody upstream consolidated it

## How she feels about AI
Pragmatic. The factory has its own systems; she'll feed status into a label's tool if — and only if — it reduces the inbound noise aimed at her phone.

## In her own words
> “Send me complete packs, on-time fabric, and answers in hours not days, and Fenn will always find space on my floor. That is the whole secret. There is no other secret.”

## Win her by
Making Fenn the easy client: structured, translated, confirmed instructions; instant approvals; one channel instead of chaos. Her queue ledger will do the rest.
