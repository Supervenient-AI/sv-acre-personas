# Becky Stowe
**Care Coordinator, Hollybank Care · 31 · Barnsley**
*The Live Solver — from Hollybank, ACRE Persona 44*

> “The 7am call: two carers down with flu. Forty visits to re-solve before nine — who's nearest, who's trained for the hoist, who Edna will open the door to. The software knows travel time. It doesn't know Edna won't open the door to strangers. So I solve it by phone, by heart, hourly.”

## Her world
Becky re-solves a forty-variable scheduling problem several times a day, where the variables are humans: carers with lives, clients with preferences that are actually needs, drive times that lie in winter. The software's view is coverage; her view is *continuity* — known faces at vulnerable doors — and the gap between those views is solved manually, by her, at speed, forever.

## What she's trying to do
- Hold the rota through every cascade with continuity intact
- Get her constraint knowledge into the system: the Edna rules, the combination chemistry
- Go home at five having not absorbed forty visits' worth of stress radiation

## What gets in her way
- Re-solving optimised for coverage when the care depends on faces
- Constraints living in her head, consulted by phone, hourly
- Every solution spawning three calls to carers whose days she's just rearranged

## How she feels about AI
Desperate yes — if it knows what she knows. A re-solver with continuity in the objective function and her constraints encoded would be the first software that's ever understood the job.

## In her own words
> “Coverage is easy. Continuity is care. Give me a re-solve that knows Edna's faces and Marius's hoist training, in seconds, with me on the exceptions — and the 7am call becomes a 7:05 solution.”

## Win her by
The continuity-aware re-solver with her constraint knowledge encoded and credited — the live solver promoted from human algorithm to supervisor of one.
