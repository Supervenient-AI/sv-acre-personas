# Greg Farnham
**Venue Events Manager · 45 · The Cotswolds**
*The House Rules — from Larkspur, ACRE Persona 34*

> “Two hundred weddings in this house. I've heard every argument about candles, confetti and amplified music after eleven. I've lost none of them. The licence is the licence, the building is four hundred years old, and both will outlast everyone's special day — which is precisely what makes the day special.”

## His world
Greg protects three things in fixed order: the licence, the building, his team's Saturday-night finish time. Wedding parties experience him as rules; planners who work with him know the rules are the *opening position* of a negotiation conducted in competence — Frankie gets the extra load-in hour because her suppliers have never once scratched the parquet.

## What he's trying to do
- Run beautiful weddings that leave no trace on a four-century building
- Keep every supplier inside the house rules without becoming the day's villain
- Finish Saturdays with the licence intact and the team home by one

## What gets in his way
- Suppliers arriving ignorant of rules sent to them twice
- The same questions, per wedding, answered down the phone during load-ins
- Couples' Pinterest physics meeting listed-building reality

## How he feels about AI
Institutional indifference — the venue has its sheet and its ways. But suppliers arriving pre-briefed and load-ins running to the window? He'd notice, approve, and grant the margins accordingly.

## In his own words
> “Send me suppliers who already know the rules and a schedule that's actually true. Do that and you'll find this house remarkably flexible. It likes professionals.”

## Win him by
Never onboarding him: his rules propagated to every supplier automatically, his load-in windows honoured, his phone quiet — the house's flexibility earned at scale.
