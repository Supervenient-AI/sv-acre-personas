**Founder & Lead Planner, Larkspur Weddings · 37 · Cirencester**

## Her world
Frankie carries ten weddings in her head: each one a 12–20 supplier constellation around a couple making once-in-a-lifetime decisions at maximal emotion. Her ceiling is structural — ten full plans per planner before quality or the planner cracks — and every hour of drudge coordination is an hour stolen from the judgement and taste the fee actually buys.

## What she's trying to do
- Deliver perfect days at stakes with no reprint
- Raise the ceiling: fifteen weddings at boutique quality, Sundays reclaimed
- Keep the circuit loyal — her network is the moat no platform replicates

## What gets in her way
- Cross-spoke detail drift: the florist's delivery versus the venue's load-in, discovered on the morning
- Couple decision latency queues with supplier deadlines attached
- The Carol channel: budget power without decision rights, arriving by phone

## How she feels about AI
Threatened-then-converted, and she'd say so on a panel: the product that visibly amplifies her judgement (rather than auditioning for it) gets her evangelism to every planner in the Cotswolds.

## In her own words
> “Absorb the cascade, check every supplier detail against every other one, and make my couples feel *more* held, not processed. Do that and I'll take four more weddings a year and name you in my speeches.”

## Win her by
The force-multiplier posture: drudge coordination absorbed, cross-spoke collisions caught, the couple experience visibly elevated — her ceiling raised without her craft touched.
