# Dawn Petty
**Head of Referrals Coordination, Oakvale · 42 · Derby**
*The Network's Memory — from Oakvale, ACRE Persona 45*

> “Ninety referring practices, and I know all their habits: who sends complete histories, who faxes — faxes! — and which insurer pays direct without a fight. The records chase is my team's whole life. A case arrived Monday for Thursday surgery; its history arrived, after four calls, Wednesday at five. We do this dance daily, with someone's dog in the middle.”

## Her world
Dawn's team converts ninety practices' chaos into a coherent surgical schedule. The sector's interoperability void — ninety practice-management systems, no standard — means every history is assembled by phone, PDF and patience. Her goodwill ledger (the free advice given, the favours owed) is the referral network's real accounting, kept nowhere.

## What she's trying to do
- Get every case's history complete before its consult — the chase industrialised
- Keep ninety referrer relationships warm: the pipeline is handoff quality plus phone manner
- Make the insurers' authorisation timing stop dictating clinical scheduling

## What gets in her way
- The records chase: another company's admin as her critical path
- Referrer drift: a practice quietly re-routing fifty cases after one slow letter, invisible until the flow data says so
- Her folklore being the operations manual, unwritten

## How she feels about AI
Day-one adopter: chasing, assembling and status-keeping is the job's whole friction, and none of it needs her warmth — which she'd rather spend on the owners and the referrers.

## In her own words
> “Chase the records tirelessly, watch the referrer flows for drift, and bank my ninety-practice folklore with my name on it. I'll keep doing the human bits — the human bits are why the network likes us.”

## Win her by
The intake pipeline (histories self-assembling, insurers tracked) and referrer-health visibility — her craft promoted from archaeology to relationships.
