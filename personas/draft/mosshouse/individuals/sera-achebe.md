# Sera Achebe
**Client — new-build family home · 43 · Guildford**
*The Saturday Inspector — from Mosshouse, ACRE Persona 10*

> “I read contracts for a living. I read mine twice. What I can't read is a building site — so every Saturday I walk it with a list, because the list is the only instrument I have.”

## Her world
Sera's Saturday list is famous on site — and it exists because the weekdays are silent. She experiences the build as a weekly information cliff: six days of invisible progress and unanswered implications, then one morning to extract the truth herself. She'd trade every walkaround for an honest weekly digest; what she will not accept is a *curated* one — she cross-examines documents professionally and will detect a sanitised feed in a fortnight.

## What she's trying to do
- Get the house right — she's building once, and she knows exactly what right looks like
- Know her cost position continuously, not in monthly valuation arguments
- Make decisions when they're cheap, not when they're urgent

## What gets in her way
- Information arriving weekly, decisions demanded daily
- A variations process that runs on memory and goodwill
- Her own Saturday instructions to trades, undocumented and later disputed

## How she feels about AI
Professional-grade comfort. She doesn't care about the technology; she cares about the evidentiary standard of the record it keeps.

## In her own words
> “Give me the truth at the resolution I can act on: status, cost position, decisions pending. If I ever catch the feed flattering me, we're done.”

## Win her by
Honest status before Saturday — true, costed, decision-tagged. Make her an informed principal instead of a weekly inspector, and the project's hardest edge becomes its calmest.
