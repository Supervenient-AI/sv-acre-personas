# Verity Lamb
**Architect · 39 · Farnham**
*The Intent Guardian — from Mosshouse, ACRE Persona 10*

> “Value engineering is fine when it happens to my face. It's the version that happens in a van, between a builder and a merchant's price list, that breaks buildings.”

## Her world
Verity drew a house worth building carefully, and spends the build watching its details face a thousand small economies. Her position is structurally awkward: allies with Daniel on quality, opponents on every variation, and dependent on a query process where the trade's improvisation is always faster than her answer. Her flagship gets one chance to be built right.

## What she's trying to do
- Get the house built as designed — or changed knowingly, in daylight, with her in the room
- Answer detail queries before the trade improvises
- Turn this project into the portfolio piece her studio's next five years need

## What gets in her way
- Queries that reach her after the concrete's poured
- Value engineering by stealth
- A fee model that makes her vigilance a charitable donation

## How she feels about AI
Cautiously positive. Her bar is precise: does the record *protect* design decisions, or does it launder their erosion? A system that flags every deviation from the drawings is her ally; one that merely documents them after the fact is a witness to the crime.

## In her own words
> “Every query to me before the improvisation, every change in front of me before the order. That's not control. That's just the building getting what it was promised.”

## Win her by
Routing detail queries to her the hour they arise, and making every drawing deviation visible before it's irreversible. Protect the intent and she'll specify the product to every builder she works with next.
