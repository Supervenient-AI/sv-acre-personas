# Daniel Moss
**MD, Mosshouse Build · 49 · Godalming**
*The Accountable Hub — from Mosshouse, ACRE Persona 10*

> “Every dispute I've ever had came down to a conversation nobody wrote down. Every single one. And I still don't write them down, because I'm building a house at the time.”

## His world
Daniel trusts handshakes, documents reluctantly and regrets it annually. His firm builds beautiful houses for exacting clients, and the line between profit and loss runs through moments like a Saturday conversation about a wider kitchen window — captured properly, it's a priced variation; lost, it's next month's argument. He bought the management app because a rival had it; the foremen half-use it; the truth still travels by phone.

## What he's trying to do
- Build well, finish on terms everyone still smiles about, and get the dinner-party referral
- Catch every change at the moment it's uttered, before it becomes a dispute
- Keep his subbies loyal — paid on time, treated straight — because they're the actual firm

## What gets in his way
- The 48-hour window between “could we just…” and a priced variation, closing silently
- A programme that's fiction by week three and a client who reads it as gospel
- His own allergy to paperwork, inherited from thirty years of being good enough not to need it

## How he feels about AI
Sceptical until it wins him one argument. The first variation dispute settled by the record instead of by goodwill will convert him permanently — and loudly, to every builder he knows.

## In his own words
> “I don't want software. I want the conversation on the scaffold to end up in the file, priced, by Monday.”

## Win him by
Variation capture from utterance to signed-and-priced in 48 hours, through the WhatsApp he already lives in. One won dispute pays for a decade of subscription.
