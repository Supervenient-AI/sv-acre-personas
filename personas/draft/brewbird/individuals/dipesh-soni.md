# Dipesh Soni
**Multi-Site Franchisee, Brewbird · 41 · Leicester**
*The Shop Steward — from Brewbird, ACRE Persona 31*

> “You want my trading data flowing up to Sheffield? Fine. What flows back down? Benchmarking I can use, or a pat on the head? I run three P&Ls. I don't do pats on the head.”

## His world
Dipesh loves the brand and audits the franchisor: every mandate gets priced against his three P&Ls, every promise gets remembered, every head-office cost gets challenged at council with receipts. His loyalty-card hack outperforms the national scheme and head office found out by accident — the network's best R&D, discovered anecdotally, which tells him everything about the centre's machinery.

## What he's trying to do
- Run three excellent cafés and maybe a fourth
- Make the franchisor earn its royalty — visibly, in services, every quarter
- Keep the network's voice organised: evidence at council beats grievance in WhatsApp

## What gets in his way
- One-way data flows dressed as partnership
- Mandates priced in Sheffield and paid in Leicester
- Innovations (his) propagating without credit (also his)

## How he feels about AI
Keen for *his* benchmarking; allergic to extraction. His reciprocity rule is the family's law: value lands at the spoke first or the data stops at the till.

## In his own words
> “Show me my Tuesdays against the network's top quartile and I'll share everything. Credit my ideas as they spread and I'll invent more. It's not complicated. It's just never been offered.”

## Win him by
Benchmarking-as-payment, innovation credit by name — converting the opposition leader into the network's loudest case study, which he'd secretly enjoy.
