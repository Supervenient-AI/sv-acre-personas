# Kirsty Lunn
**Practice Manager, The Arches Clinic · 36 · Sale**
*The Federation's Operations — from Arches, ACRE Persona 43*

> “Fifteen businesses share this building, this diary and this reputation — and I run the shared bits with colour-coding and goodwill. The insurer authorisations alone are a part-time job. The CQC binder is another. The diary politics are a third. I have one job title.”

## Her world
Kirsty operates the seams the patients never see: fourteen self-employed diaries reconciled, billing split per associate per insurer, authorisations chased per patient per session-limit, and a compliance estate assembled across six regulatory regimes for one inspection. Her systems are personal, excellent and unscalable — the federation's operations are, functionally, her.

## What she's trying to do
- Keep fifteen businesses' shared surface running without visible seams
- Automate the authorisation drag — sessions stalled on codes are patients stalled in pain
- Make inspection-readiness continuous: the binder assembling itself from work already done

## What gets in her way
- Insurer portals built to discourage their own use
- Compliance documents arriving from fourteen associates at fourteen standards
- Being the single human instance of the operating system (the Gracie Lim condition, clinical edition)

## How she feels about AI
Immediate adopter: authorisation chasing and compliance assembly are her sinkholes, and neither needs her humanity. The diary politics, she'll keep — those need a face.

## In her own words
> “Chase the codes, build the binder, reconcile the splits. I'll handle the humans. The humans are the part I'm good at, when the portals leave me time for them.”

## Win her by
Authorisation automation and continuous compliance assembly — the federation's operations given machinery, its operator given her job back.
