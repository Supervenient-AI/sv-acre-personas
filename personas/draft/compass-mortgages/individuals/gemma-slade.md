# Gemma Slade
**Client — self-employed remortgager · 35 · Kings Heath**
*The Stalled Beneficiary — from Compass, ACRE Persona 21*

> “I know it's me holding it up. I know. The bank statements are somewhere, the accountant's on it, and every email about it makes me feel like I'm failing a test I'm paying to sit.”

## At a glance
| | |
|---|---|
| Age | 35 |
| Lives | Kings Heath, Birmingham — partner, a dog, a Victorian terrace whose fixed rate ends in nine weeks |
| Background | Freelance graphic designer, six years self-employed; good income, chaotic evidence of it |
| Situation | Remortgaging before a rate cliff; self-employed paperwork doing what self-employed paperwork does |
| Emotional state | A low-grade hum of financial shame entirely disproportionate to her actual finances |
| Channels | WhatsApp (responds in minutes, often with a photo of a document at 9pm), email (where requests go to be dreaded) |

## Her world
Gemma is the chain's most innocent bottleneck: the person the process exists to serve, stalling it from a mix of overwhelm, opaque requests and the special dread of self-employed paperwork. When a checklist is clear and kind, she responds in minutes; when an email lists six documents in lender jargon, she goes quiet for nine days and hates herself about it.

## What she's trying to do
- Secure the rate before the cliff — the deadline she knows about vaguely and fears specifically
- Get through this without feeling audited by her own side
- Never think about mortgages again for five years

## What gets in her way
- Requests in jargon, batched into overwhelm
- Her accountant's geological response times
- The shame spiral that converts confusion into silence

## How she feels about AI
Doesn't care what's behind the message. Cares that it's one thing at a time, in plain words, kind in tone, and fine with a 9pm photo of a crumpled SA302.

## In her own words
> “Tell me exactly what to send, one thing at a time, and don't make it weird. I'll send it from the sofa tonight.”

## Win her by
Warm, judgement-free, channel-native chasing — one clear ask at a time, photographed documents welcomed, the deadline handled invisibly on her behalf.
