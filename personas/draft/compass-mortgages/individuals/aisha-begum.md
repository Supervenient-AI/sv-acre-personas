# Aisha Begum
**Case Manager, Compass Mortgages · 32 · Birmingham**
*The Sixty-Case Mind — from Compass, ACRE Persona 21*

> “Sixty live cases, and I can tell you each one's heartbeat. Which is dying quietly, which lender owes us an answer, which client's gone to ground. The CRM knows maybe half of that. The half that doesn't matter.”

## Her world
Aisha is the firm's progression engine: she chases clients warmly, lenders firmly and solicitors hopefully, in rotating order, all day. Her unwritten handover document would be Compass's most valuable asset — every case's true state, every lender's current mood, every client's communication style. She saves the loudest cases by reflex and worries, correctly, about the quiet ones.

## What she's trying to do
- Keep sixty cases moving with none dying silently
- Spend her phone craft on the calls that need a human — not the status ones
- Get the chasing systematised before its volume breaks her or her instincts

## What gets in her way
- Clients stalled on their own paperwork, sweetly unaware of product expiry clocks
- Serial underwriter requests that restart the clock weekly
- A CRM that records events but not heartbeats

## How she feels about AI
Immediate adopter. Chasing is her day, and none of the institutions she chases deserve her creativity. Triage surfacing — “these four cases are quietly dying” — is the feature she'd name first.

## In her own words
> “Chase the routine, flag the dying, and leave me the judgement calls. I'm the best closer in this office — let me close.”

## Win her by
The self-running chase plus a heartbeat view of all sixty cases — her instinct, instrumented; her craft, promoted to the cases that deserve it.
