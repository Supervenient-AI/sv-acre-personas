# Fiona Drummond
**Partner, Northbeam Advisory · 51**
*The Pillar Owner — from Northbeam, ACRE Persona 6*

> “I care about three things: utilisation, quality, and never appearing in a leakage incident report. Two of those are spreadsheets. The third is the one that keeps me up.”

## Her world
Fiona's relationships predate and outlast every engagement — Helen Voss takes her calls because of a programme they survived together in 2019. She experiences the firm's operational reality (two laptops, access sprawl, knowledge silos) only as occasional partner-meeting agenda items, until the day one of them threatens a client relationship. Then it has her full attention, briefly and decisively.

## What she's trying to do
- Keep utilisation high and quality higher — premium rates need both
- Protect her client relationships from everything, including her own firm's entropy
- Never, ever be the partner in the leakage post-mortem

## What gets in her way
- Quality erosion she can smell but can't measure: the obviously-AI deck reaching a client at premium rates
- Operational risk she can't see until audit season
- Pitches that talk features when she buys outcomes

## How she feels about AI
Outcome-only. She will sponsor anything that demonstrably reduces risk or raises margin, and read none of the documentation.

## In her own words
> “Tell me the risk number went down and the margin went up, in one sentence I could repeat to a general counsel. That's the business case.”

## Win her by
A one-sentence risk posture she can say at a client dinner, and a quality bar that catches workslop before clients see it. Sponsorship follows; details never required.
